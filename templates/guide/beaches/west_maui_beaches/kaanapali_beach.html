Ka'anapali Beach

Ka'anapali Beach / "Black Rock"

Very long beach that stretches in front of the resorts. Our favorite area of this beach (and one of our favorite spots in all of Maui) is Black Rock, which is on the north end of the beach behind the Sheraton Resort. Black Rock offers great snorkeling with lots of fish, turtles, moray eels and we’ve even come across spotted eagle rays and even a white tip reef shark.

Here's a video we took while snorkeling Black Rock:

There's a nice paved walking path that stretches the length of this beach along the resorts with the Whaler's Village shopping center in between the resorts.

Parking can be a little hard to come by and we try to park in the Sheraton beach access parking lot (only about 12 spots) or the small Ka’anapali Beach Hotel lot (about 6 spots) for easy access to Black Rock. So try to get here early (before 9:00am) to get a spot.
