Keawakapu Beach

Long stretch of beach just south of the Kihei Boat ramp. Snorkeling is good on the north side of the beach, the sandy bottom makes for easy entry and exit and at times the south end of the beach has a large tide pool area.

TIf you were thinking of renting a kayak or stand up paddle board, this is a good area to do so because the Surf Shack is right next to the beach and located on the bottom floor of the Mana Kai Maui resort. Prices are reasonable and staff is friendly.

There are a couple of parking areas for Keawakapu Beach. One is the public parking in front of the Mana Kai Maui Resort, another is at the intersection of S. Kihei Rd and Kilohana Dr (there's beach access across the street from this lot that takes you between a couple of multi-million dollar homes, just look for the blue beach access sign) and the third parking area is at the end of S. Kihei Rd (there's also some off street parking on S. Kihei Rd just before you get to this parking lot). Restrooms and showers are located at the Mana Kai beach area and the end of S. Kihei Rd parking lot.
