Po'olenalena Beach

Awesome beach w/ great snorkeling on either side and when the surf's up, body boarding is great, especially the south side. This is a large beach that is a lot less crowded than Wailea Beach. There are a couple of porta-potties but no showers at the main parking lot and there is a small (6-8 spaces) parking spot at the Makena Surf that will have a shower, but no restrooms. Since we enjoy the south end of this beach more, we usually park at the smaller parking lot.

The shoreline makes a great place to sit and watch for whales or view sunsets. The water here is not the best for swimming, however at the south end of the park is "The Cove," which is a small beach area that is a very popular surfing spot. The small waves here makes this the ideal place for beginner surfers and there are a couple of companies right across the street from here that offer surfing lessons.
