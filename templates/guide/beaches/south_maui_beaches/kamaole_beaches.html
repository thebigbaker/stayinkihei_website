Kamaole Beaches

Broken down into three separate beaches (Kam I, Kam II and Kam III) these beaches have great soft sand, great swimming, good snorkeling, and at times good waves for body boarding. All three of the Kam Beaches have lifeguards, restrooms, showers and charcoal grills.

Kam I is across the street from our Pacific Shores condo and Kam II is directly across the street from our Kihei Kai Nani condo. Kam I is the larger of the three beaches and has good snorkeling around the rocks at each end of the beach. Snorkeling is great on either end of Kam II, but we prefer the south end (left side that separates Kam II and III) a little better.

Kam III has a huge lawn which can get crowded on the weekends (It seems like every weekend there is a birthday party w/ multiple jumping castles on the Kam III lawn!). If waves are coming in, Kam III also is the best body board spot of the three Kams.
