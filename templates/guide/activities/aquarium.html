Maui Ocean Center and Aquarium

Maui Ocean Center and Aquarium

Located in Ma'alea, the aquarium only houses species that are found in Hawaiian waters. Open from 9am - 5pm, it's about $23 per adult for a day pass and $34 for weekly passes. If you have kids, the weekly pass is a great idea. Our daughter loves the aquarium and we visit the aquarium often.

Among the exhibits are the tide pool, turtle lagoon, hammerhead shark exhibit and the large aquarium w/ a walk through tunnel that houses different rays, large fish, and several large sharks (last time we were there they had 2 tiger sharks!).
